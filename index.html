About

project

La Ferme-Mart Web

La ferme-mart is a collaborative project as part of the government program called Independent Study or commonly known as (MSIB) Kampus Merdeka. It is an e-commerce website developed to help individual farm owners expand their market, reduce costs, and facilitate transactions.

Visualization of (KPIs) in a yearly period

Explanation Based on Pictures

1. Sum Yearly Sales Person Performance:
Shows total sales performance per salesperson by ProductKey from 2017-2020. High sales indicate strong performance, while low sales suggest a need for training or support.

2. Average Yearly Sales:
Displays average sales per product. High sales products (e.g., ProductKey 564 and 561) perform well, while low sales products (e.g., ProductKey 559 and 555) need improvement.
